== I AM NOT ALCOHOLIC

It is direct competitor of http://beeradvocate.com

I am not alcoholic - is a catalog of beer-like products.
Users can create records of new kinds of beer and share their reviews.

Also, there's a number of killer features:

1. Availability map
2. Rating and pricing graphs
3. SMS notifications when someone calls your favourite beer a 'piss'
4. And more =)
